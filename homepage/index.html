    Progressive enhancement is what allows for a website to be structured based on its content and to be styled directly by its trained user. I feel that this is important in a modern web development world due to its ability to be a true user centered way of data implementation. It allows users to dictate what they use and style their sites off of based on their comfort with the multiple layers of software. https://www.smashingmagazine.com/2009/04/progressive-enhancement-what-it-is-and-how-to-use-it/ I found this insightful due to it breaking down each layer in depth.
